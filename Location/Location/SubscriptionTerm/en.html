Member In-Purchase Instructions



Confirm subscription:  After the user confirms the purchase and payment, it will be credited to the iTunes account, and the one-time payment is for lifetime use.


Restore in-app purchase: After uninstalling and reinstalling, the user clicks the "Restore Purchase" button to help you restore VIP benefits. If you change the device and reinstall, you need to log in to the same appid and then click "Restore Purchase" to restore the benefits.


Manage subscription:Users can manage or cancel in-app purchases in the iTunes & App Store account management in the phone system settings.
