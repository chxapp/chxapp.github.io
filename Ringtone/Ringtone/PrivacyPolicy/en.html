1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Audio Files: When you use our ringtone creation features, we access audio files stored on your device (music library, voice recordings, or imported files) solely to enable ringtone creation. These files remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email for support, we collect your email address and any information you choose to provide in your message.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as IDFA), language settings, screen resolution, IP address, network type, and mobile carrier information.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data including feature usage, crash logs, performance data, and diagnostic information to help us improve the App.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) for advertising and analytics purposes, subject to your consent where required by applicable law.

1.3 Permissions We Request

  • Media Library Access: To import music, audio files, and ringtones from your device. This is essential for the core functionality of creating ringtones.
  • Notifications: To send you alerts about app updates and feature announcements.
  • Network Access: To serve advertisements and enable analytics services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core Services: Enable ringtone creation from your audio files, including importing, editing, trimming, and setting ringtones.
  • To Improve Our App: Analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and enhance user experience.
  • For Advertising: Display advertisements within the App to support our free service. We use your advertising identifier to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.
  • For Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To Communicate: Respond to your support inquiries and send important updates about the App.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We engage third-party service providers for analytics, advertising, and crash reporting. These providers may collect device information and usage data. They are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

3.3 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your audio files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law.

Please understand that no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access and Portability: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of Tracking: On iOS, you can reset or disable your advertising identifier in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. You can also limit ad tracking on a per-app basis.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke app permissions (such as Media Library access) at any time through your device Settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and notify you through the App or other means. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
